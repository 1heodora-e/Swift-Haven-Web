Understanding Your Cycle Phases

Your menstrual cycle consists of four distinct phases. Here's how to track them:

  • Menstrual Phase (Days 1-5): Starts with your period
  • Follicular Phase (Days 6-14): Begins after your period ends
  • Ovulation Phase (Days 14-16): Mid-cycle
  • Luteal Phase (Days 17-28): Post-ovulation until next period

Note: These are average durations. Your cycle might vary, and that's completely normal!
